# Liquidity Pools

![](<../.gitbook/assets/pool-overview.png>)

The Pool page lets you provide liquidity to earn a share of all swap fees generated by the pair. JinkFi supports both **Uniswap V2** (full-range) and **Uniswap V3** (concentrated range) liquidity.

---

## V2 Pools

V2 pools use a constant product formula (`x · y = k`). Liquidity is spread evenly across all possible prices.

**How to add V2 liquidity:**

1. Navigate to **Pool → V2**
2. Select **Token A** and **Token B**
3. Enter the amount for one token — the other is calculated automatically
4. Click **Supply** and confirm two transactions (approve + add)

![](<../.gitbook/assets/pool-v2-add.png>)

**What you receive:** LP tokens representing your share of the pool. These are standard ERC-20 tokens you can hold, transfer, or stake in the [Farm](farm.md).

---

## V3 Pools (Concentrated Liquidity)

V3 lets you concentrate your liquidity within a custom price range, earning a larger share of fees when the price trades within your range.

![](<../.gitbook/assets/pool-v3-range.png>)

**How to add V3 liquidity:**

1. Navigate to **Pool → V3 Position**
2. Select the token pair and fee tier (0.05%, 0.3%, or 1%)
3. Set your **price range** (lower and upper tick)
4. Enter the amount and click **Add Liquidity**

{% hint style="info" %}
**Choosing a fee tier:**
- **0.05%** — stablecoins (USDC/USDT, pathUSD/USDC.e)
- **0.3%** — most standard pairs
- **1%** — exotic or low-liquidity pairs
{% endhint %}

{% hint style="warning" %}
If the current price moves outside your range, your position stops earning fees and becomes 100% composed of one token. You will need to rebalance or close the position.
{% endhint %}

---

## My Positions

The **My Positions** tab shows all your active V3 positions:

- Current price vs your range
- Uncollected fees
- Total position value

![](<../.gitbook/assets/pool-positions.png>)

Click **Collect Fees** to claim accumulated swap fees without closing the position.

---

## Removing Liquidity

### V2
Go to **Pool → My Positions → V2**, select the pair and percentage to remove, then click **Remove**.

### V3
Open the position, click **Remove Liquidity**, select the percentage, and confirm. Fees are collected automatically on removal.

---

## APR Calculation

APR is estimated from the pool's 24-hour volume and total TVL:

```
APR ≈ (volume24h × feeTier × 365) / TVL
```

High-volume, low-TVL pools have the highest APR.
