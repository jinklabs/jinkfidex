# Provide Liquidity (V2)

This guide walks you through adding liquidity to a Uniswap V2 pool on JinkFi to earn a share of swap fees.

---

## Prerequisites

- Wallet connected to Tempo
- Equal value of **both tokens** you want to deposit
- USD for gas

---

## Step 1 — Go to Pool → V2

Click **Pool** in the sidebar, then select the **V2** tab.

---

## Step 2 — Select a Token Pair

![](<../.gitbook/assets/guide-v2-select.png>)

Click **Add Liquidity** and select:

1. **Token A** — first token of the pair
2. **Token B** — second token of the pair

If a pool for this pair doesn't exist yet, JinkFi will prompt you to create it.

---

## Step 3 — Enter Amounts

Type the amount for one token. The other amount is calculated automatically to maintain the current pool ratio.

{% hint style="info" %}
V2 pools require you to deposit both tokens at the current price ratio. If you only have one token, swap half of it first.
{% endhint %}

---

## Step 4 — Approve and Supply

![](<../.gitbook/assets/guide-v2-supply.png>)

1. Click **Approve** for each token (one-time per token, first deposit only)
2. Click **Supply**
3. Confirm the transaction in your wallet

---

## Step 5 — Receive LP Tokens

After the transaction confirms, you receive **LP tokens** in your wallet representing your share of the pool. Hold them to keep earning fees, or deposit them in a [Farm](../features/farm.md) to earn additional JINK rewards.

---

## Removing V2 Liquidity

1. Go to **Pool → My Positions → V2**
2. Select the pair
3. Choose the percentage to remove
4. Click **Remove** and confirm

Your tokens are returned to your wallet proportional to your pool share.
